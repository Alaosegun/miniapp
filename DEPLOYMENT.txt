ROCK PAPER SCISSORS MINI APP - DEPLOYMENT INSTRUCTIONS
========================================================

IMPORTANT STEPS TO COMPLETE BEFORE DEPLOYMENT:

1. UPDATE FARCASTER.JSON
   - Open public/.well-known/farcaster.json
   - Replace "https://your-domain.com" with your actual deployed domain
   - Replace "0x" in baseBuilder.ownerAddress with your Base wallet address
   - Update all image URLs (iconUrl, splashImageUrl, screenshotUrls, etc.) with actual hosted images

2. GENERATE ACCOUNT ASSOCIATION
   - Deploy your app to production first
   - Go to https://www.base.dev/ (Base Build Account association tool)
   - Enter your domain and follow instructions to generate accountAssociation credentials
   - Update the accountAssociation fields in farcaster.json with the generated values

3. UPDATE EMBED METADATA
   - Open index.html
   - Update the fc:miniapp meta tag with your actual domain and image URL

4. UPDATE PAYMENT ADDRESS
   - Open src/components/Game.jsx
   - Find the handlePayment function
   - Replace '0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000000' with your treasury wallet address

5. BUILD FOR PRODUCTION
   - Run: npm run build
   - Deploy the 'dist' folder to your hosting service (Vercel, Netlify, etc.)

6. TEST YOUR APP
   - Use Base Build Preview tool: https://www.base.dev/preview
   - Validate embeds and account association
   - Test the launch functionality

7. PUBLISH
   - Create a post in the Base app with your app's URL
   - Your mini app will be live!
